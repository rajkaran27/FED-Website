My experience in SP

I have been in Singapore Polytechnic for two years as I took the Polytechnic Foundation Program (PFP) after my N-Levels. I have been enjoying my time here as there are a lot of food options as SP has six foodcourts and an abundance of space to study. The lecturers and materials provided helps me learn better.

My CCA Experience

I joined SP's Hockey when I entered year 1. I wanted to pick up a new sport and SP's Hockey stood out to me the most. I have been in the CCA since April and I am enjoying it so far. Trainings has been enjoyable so far as I have learnt so much from experienced seniors and teammates. I am looking forward to the POL-ITE tournament as I would love to represent Singapore Polytechnic.
